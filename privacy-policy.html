Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

Robert John Ulep ("I", "me", or "my") operates the My Piggery Farm Manager mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

Information I Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, I collect:

  • Email address
  • Full name
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Farm name and location

Farm Data

When you use the App, I store:

  • Pig inventory records (tags, names, breeds, weights, etc.)
  • Financial transactions (expenses and income)
  • Farm profile information

Technical Information

I automatically collect:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Sync timestamps

How I Use Your Information

I use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Send password reset emails when requested
  • Improve the App

Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely on servers hosted on Fly.io
  • I use industry-standard encryption for data transmission
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Your farm data is isolated and only accessible to you

Data Retention

  • Your data is retained as long as your account is active
  • You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting me

Third-Party Services

I use the following third-party services:

  • Fly.io - Server hosting
  • Supabase - Database hosting

These services have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. I do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. I will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the App.
